General Eligibility Guidelines for Child Health Care Schemes

  • Indian citizenship is required for all government child health care schemes.
  • Age criteria vary by scheme but typically cover children from birth to 6 years.
  • Income criteria may apply - Below Poverty Line (BPL) families often receive priority.
  • Birth certificate or Aadhaar card is required for registration.
  • Vaccination records may be required for immunization programs.
  • Anganwadi registration is necessary for nutrition-related schemes.
  • Special provisions are available for girl children in many schemes.
