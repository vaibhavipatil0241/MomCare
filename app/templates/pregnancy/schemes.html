General Eligibility Guidelines

  • Indian citizenship is required for most government schemes and benefits.
  • Income criteria may apply - check specific scheme requirements for income limits.
  • Age restrictions may apply for certain schemes (typically 18+ for maternity benefits).
  • Valid identity documents (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID) are required for application.
  • Bank account details are necessary for direct benefit transfer (DBT) schemes.
  • Medical certificates may be required for health-related schemes and benefits.
  • Apply through official government portals or authorized centers to avoid fraud.
