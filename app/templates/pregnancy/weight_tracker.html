Weight Gain Guidelines

  • Underweight (BMI <18.5): 12.7-18.1 kg
  • Normal weight (BMI 18.5-24.9): 11.3-15.9 kg
  • Overweight (BMI 25-29.9): 6.8-11.3 kg
  • Obese (BMI ≥30): 5.0-9.1 kg

Healthy Weight Gain Tips

  • Eat frequent, small meals throughout the day
  • Focus on nutrient-dense foods
  • Include lean proteins in every meal
  • Stay hydrated with water and milk
  • Listen to your hunger cues

When to Seek Help

  • Gaining less than 1kg/month after 1st trimester
  • Gaining more than 3kg/month consistently
  • Sudden weight changes (+2kg/week)
  • Concerns about nutrition or appetite
  • Any rapid swelling or bloating
